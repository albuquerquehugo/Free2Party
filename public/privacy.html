Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to Free2Party. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Free2Party uses Firebase Authentication to manage your account. This includes your email address and profile name. We also store event data and preferences you create within the app using Firebase Firestore.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app: managing events, tracking budgets, and syncing your data across your devices.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption through Firebase services to protect your data. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at hello@free2party.app.
