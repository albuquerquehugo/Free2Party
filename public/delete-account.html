Account Deletion & Data Removal

Last Updated: May 2026

At Free2Party, we respect your right to privacy and give you full control over your data. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to delete your account

The fastest way to delete your account is directly through the Free2Party mobile app:

  1. Open the Free2Party app.
  2. Go to your Profile screen.
  3. Tap the More Options icon (three dots) in the top right corner.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your choice in the dialog. For security reasons, you may be asked to log in again if your session is old.

What data is deleted?

When you delete your account, the following information is permanently and immediately removed from our servers:

  • Profile Information: Name, email address, bio, birthday, and gender.
  • Social Data: Your friends list, blocked users, and pending friend requests.
  • Content: All scheduled plans and event notes.
  • Media: Your profile picture is permanently deleted from our storage.
  • Authentication: Your login credentials and account access.

Note: This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Manual deletion request

If you are unable to access the app or wish to request deletion manually, please email us at hello@free2party.app from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Data Retention

We do not retain any of your personal data after the account is deleted. Only aggregated, non-identifiable technical logs may be kept for a limited period to ensure system stability, but these cannot be linked back to you.
