Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: May 2026

Free2Party is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for any content or behavior that endangers children, specifically Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

We strictly prohibit the sharing, promotion, or solicitation of any material that depicts child sexual abuse or exploitation. Any account found to be involved in such activities will be immediately terminated and reported to the relevant authorities.

2. In-App Reporting

Free2Party provides built-in reporting tools. Users can report any profile or content that violates our safety standards by clicking the "Report" button. These reports are prioritized and reviewed by our team.

3. Content Moderation

We use a combination of automated filters and manual review to identify and remove inappropriate content. Reports of CSAM/CSAE are investigated immediately.

4. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

We comply with all regional and national laws regarding child safety. We cooperate with the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and other law enforcement agencies by reporting illegal content and providing necessary information for investigations.

5. Contact and Compliance

Our designated point of contact for child safety concerns and compliance is ready to address any issues. If you have questions or need to report a concern directly, please contact us at:

support@free2party.app
